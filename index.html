Quickberry Quackberry Pick Me A Blackberry! This map and project is inspired by the iconic children's book Jamberry by Bruce Degen. As a child, this was one of my favorite books. I remember thinking that the berry illustrations were some of the most delicious things I'd ever seen. The background of this page is inspired by Degen's drawing style. A simple idea, absolutely obliterated by what I consider to be genius illustrations accompanied by clever rhymes. Here's to Jamberry, the fantastical and whimsical odyssey of a homely bear and boy.
